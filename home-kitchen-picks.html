👩‍🍳 Instructions

Never serve undercooked chicken again with this foolproof recipe!

🛒 Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken
  • 4 tbsp butter softened
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • Fresh rosemary & thyme
  • 1 lemon sliced
  • Salt and pepper

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Rub chicken with butter & herbs
  2. Stuff with lemon & garlic
  3. Roast at 425°F for 90 min
  4. Check temp reaches 165°F
  5. Rest 10 min before serving
