Crunchy, golden chicken wings prepared with crisp spices and zero heavy oils.

🛒 Pantry Ingredients

  • 2 lbs fresh chicken wings
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika & onion powder
  • Coarse sea salt & black pepper

👩‍🍳 Step-by-Step

  2. Arrange inside the fryer basket at 400°F.
